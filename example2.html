The Art and Science of Fireworks

You can use effects fuses in aerial shells, in rockets (including my favorite Stinger Missile Rockets), in mines, and many other fireworks. Heck, it’s even fun to just light a 4″ piece and throw it in the air –especially with the crackling sound the flying fish fuses make. You may have seen flying fish. Up in the air, they appear to kind of “swim around” in the night sky. (My neighbor’s kid calls ‘em “spermies!”). This is cool stuff! One of my favorite firework “ingredients.”
